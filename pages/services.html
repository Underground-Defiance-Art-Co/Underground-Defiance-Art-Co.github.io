Our Services

What We Offer

Along with collective studio spaces, Underground Defiance offers many different events and workshops that are open and accessible to anyone 18+. We also offer after school on Tuesday and Thursday nights for elementary through high school, because we believe that every child deserves to be able to artistically express themselves, we just help them figure out how.

  • Studio Spaces
    Here at Underground Defiance we take immense pride to offer free accessible spaces for all artistic disciplines. All of our spaces are equipped with lights on dimmer switches, which is for a multitude of accessibility reasons.
    • Dance: We have three dance studios with wall mounted grab bars, tactical floor markers, mirrors, audio systems, yoga mats and supportive pillows. Also, all of our dance studios are equipped rigging and other equipment for aerial and pole.
    • Music: We have two recording booths (don't worry, they are built for people with mobility devices or service dogs to be able to navigate), and one production studio. We offer a wide range of instruments including, standard and accessible keyboards, string, wind, percussion, brass, electric instruments.
    • Theater: We have a large stage with a ramp, ample backstage space, tactile floor markers, and pathway lights.
    • Visual Art: We have one big art studio equipped with standard and adaptive supplies for painting, drawing, sculpting, and ceramics. All of our supplies are organized into pull-down shelving so supplies can be accessed from a seated position. All supplies are labeled with braille.
    • Writing: We have a variety of writing utensils and devices, as well as scribes for those who want to dictate. Our writing nook is equipped with adjustable tables and various different places to sit.
