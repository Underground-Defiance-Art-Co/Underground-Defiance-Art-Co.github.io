Programs & Events

Stay up to date with our latest programs, workshops, and events! Whether you're an artist, art lover, or supporter, there's always something happening at Underground Defiance Art Collective.

Upcoming Events

  • Defiant Artists Open Mic
    Date: August 24, 2025
    Location: The Lavender Room, Main Gallery Space
    Details: Join us for a night of spoken word, live painting, music, and community! Open to all forms of creative expression. Sign-ups at the door.
  • Street Art Stencil Workshop
    Date: September 14, 2025
    Location: Outdoor Courtyard
    Details: Learn the basics of making and using stencils from local street artists. All supplies provided. RSVP required.
  • Queer Artists' Networking Night
    Date: October 2, 2025
    Location: The Lavender Room
    Details: An evening to connect, share, and collaborate. All LGBTQIA+ artists and allies welcome. Refreshments served.

Ongoing Programs

  • Community Mural Project
    Every Saturday, 12–4pm
    Location: Mural Alley
    Details: Help create a collaborative mural that represents our collective's spirit. All skill levels welcome!
  • Art for All – Free Supplies Pantry
    Open during gallery hours
    Location: Lobby
    Details: Need art supplies? Take what you need, leave what you can. Donations always appreciated.
  • Monthly Gallery Exhibition
    First Friday of every month
    Location: Main Gallery
    Details: Rotating selection of works by collective and guest artists. Free and open to the public.

Interested in volunteering, performing, or leading a workshop? Contact us to get involved!
